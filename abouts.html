THE DUCK HOUSE

At Bun Mang Vit Thanh Da – The Duck House, guests can enjoy the comforting taste of traditional Vietnamese duck dishes. The signature Bun Mang Vit features tender duck, crunchy bamboo shoots, and a rich, flavorful broth. The menu also includes a variety of satisfying options such as rice dishes, noodle soups, and other familiar Vietnamese favorites. Simple, authentic, and full of flavor, it’s a great spot to enjoy a hearty meal any day. 🍜🦆
